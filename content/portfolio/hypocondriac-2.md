„Hüpohondrik“ on osa üheksa kunstniku (Sandra Jõgeva, Mai Sööt, Tiina Sööt, Alla Ting, Epp Kubu ja Olivia Verrev Eestist, Julischka Stengele Saksamaalt ning Teresa Novotny ja Cizzy Conzales Austriast) ühisest terviklikust performance`ist "How to properly love/destroy something".
See etendusViinis LABfactorys, Dresdenis friedrichstadt Zentralis ja Berliinis  Flutgrabenis 2011.a. märtsis, lisaks 2011a. augustis Polymeris Kultuuritehase Festivalil.
 "Hypocondriac" is a part of a performance by nine female artists (Sandra Jõgeva, Mai Sööt, Tiina Sööt, Alla Ting, Epp Kubu and Olivia Verrev from Estonia, Julischka Stengele from Germany ,Teresa Novotny and Cizzy Conzales from Austria) titled "How to properly love/destroy something". It was performed in LABfactory in Vienna, friedrichstadt Zentral in Dresden and in Flutgraben in Berlin in March 2011. An additional performance was given at Culture Factory Festival in Polymer in Tallinn in August 2011.
