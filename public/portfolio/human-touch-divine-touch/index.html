Solo exhibition at Grace Exhibition Space, Brooklyn, New York June 2009 Isiknäitus galeriis Grace Exhibition Space New Yorgis 2009.a. juunis Photo: Krista Sokolova   Press release: SANDRA JOGEVA [TALLINN, ESTONIA] “HUMAN TOUCH, DIVINE TOUCH” Friday, June 12 - Friday, June 19 1:00 - 8:00 PM Thursday - Sunday   PERFORMANCE ART and INSTALLATION by SANDRA JOGEVA Religion and S&M are the subject matters that Sandra Jogeva explores through her art. It’s about submitting yourself for an higher power. It could be a Dominatrix or God Himself. It is about being a deviant in society through one’s religious beliefs or through sexual orientation. The installation with performances, “Human Touch, Divine Touch” are based on Sandra Jogeva’s experiences as a professional Dominatrix over two years: I am the Domina, you are the customer. I’m the psychologist, you’re the client. I’m your marriage consultant, the symbol of your domineering or harsh mother (whom you desire, but cannot have, for you’re not allowed to touch). But when you experience physical pain your brain releases endorphins and adrenalin and due to the combined effect of these chemicals you feel very much present and alive, and above the problems that usually prey on you. As nature intended. And therefore you spend your lunch hour in my company. After that you go to your office and feel wonderfully relaxed and confident. Happy. I will never again connect with people on such a basis. At which I even feel slightly sorry.

  1. “Vox Dei: Karaoke Version”. 2007. ca 3 min. I was quite interested briefly in religious cults and obscure Christian congregations. A family in southern Estonia tried to save my soul using “speaking in tongues” as their method. My recording is transcribed into the international phonetic alphabet.
