Keskeakriis (Middle Life Crisis) 2012 Camera: Peeter Ülevain (Tallinn TV) Editing: Tanel V Kulla Assistants: Jane Jagna Paadimeister, Veronika Pikkas Special thanks: Katrin Essenson A stand up tragedy in Estonian about middle life crisis amd the position of actors as opinion leaders in Estonian media. Performed at the festival titled Maikellukese päevad at Von Krahl Theatre in Tallinn, Estonia.
