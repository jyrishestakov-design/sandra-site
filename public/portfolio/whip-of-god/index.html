Whip of God/ Jumala roosk

2012

Diverse Universe III, Lahti, Finland The performance was about 15 minutes long and was dealing with marginal ecstasy of both S /M and belonging to a obscure religious organization. It included me telling my story of being a dominatrix for 1,5 years, offering the audience a possibility to receive the free S/M public session (which nobody wanted), using the sound of the religious practice called “speaking in tongues” and “smoking out the audience” at the end of a performance. 2007 Jumala roosk Performance festivalil Diverse Universe III Lahtis.
